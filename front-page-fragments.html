2015 Evolution School, February 21

The Arizona Border Region will be hosing an Evolution Performance Driving Phase One School on Saturday, February 21st at Marana Regional Airport. This program is great for anyone from seasoned veterans to complete novice driver, and features 24 runs with our team of National Caliber instructors. Our Phase One will cover car placement, car control, as well as an intensive exercise on visualization skills. Cost for the school is $260.
