Rallycross Site & Entry Fee Information

Event Site: Motoland Mx Park

Motoland Mx Park is located at 6734 S. Sisler Rd, Casa Grande, AZ. Note, however, that Google maps will navigate you to the wrong entrance. It is best to enter lat/long coordinates 32.821160, -111.799972 in order to navigate directly to the track entrance.
