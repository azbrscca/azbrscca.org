Rallycross Site & Entry Fee Information

Event Site: MC Motorpark/Musselman Honda Circuit

MC Motorpark/Musselman Honda Circuit is located at 11700 S Harrison Rd. Use the North Entrance. Once on site follow the orange lines till you reach the star area and park.

The SCCA requires that all event participants be SCCA members. If you are not an SCCA member a weekend membership is available for an extra $10, which is included in the Non SCCA entry fees shown above.
