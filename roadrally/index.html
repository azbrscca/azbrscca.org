Road Rally

On Feb 7/8 the Arizona Region will present the Don Quixote Regional Tour and the Sancho Panza Regional Tour. There will also be the Milling Around Regional Course and the Valley of the Sun Regional Course.

Headquarters will be the Quality Inn in Goodyear/Avondale at 950 N Dysart Rd. Use Exit 128 (Dysart Rd) of I-10. This is about 25 miles west of Phoenix Airport.

The Tour rallyes are not boring and very scenic. Sunrise Feb 7 713 Sunset 603

The course rallyes are maybe 6 or 7 out of 10 for difficulty. Tour Rallyes have 6 passage controls and 1 DIYC each. Course rallyes have about 14 or so DIYC controls each.

Contact John Getchell at jgetchell@cox.net for more info.

Sat AM apx. 58 mi 9-12; PM apx 70 mi 1-350 Lunch between rallyes (ARBY’s/BURGER KING) Sun AM apx. 55 miles 9-12; PM apx. 36 mi 1-4

A thorough understanding of the RRR’s and event generals will keep your score Under 200 (50 point max on course, 20 on Tours). No unpaved roads will be used.

AZ Opera will present Eugene Onegin (Tchaikovsky) on Saturday evening. See Azopera.org for more info.
